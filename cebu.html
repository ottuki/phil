ALL ABOUT BEACHES IN CEBU

Located in Central Visayas, Cebu province is another popular entry point to the Philippines aside from Manila, especially for those heading to popular destinations in the Philippines located in the Visayas region like Boracay and Bohol. Aside from its ideal location for first-time visitors in the Philippines, Cebu also has the nickname "The Gateway to a Thousand Journeys" since it is home to nearly 170 islands that offer a wide array of activities you can try and places to visit for your Cebu tourist spots itinerary. When it comes to Cebu beaches, some of the must-visit destinations include Bantayan Island, Sumilon Island, and Malapascua Island.
