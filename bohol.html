ALL ABOUT THE BEACHES IN BOHOL

Bohol's beaches offer a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and natural beauty. Whether you're seeking vibrant nightlife or serene spots to unwind, Bohol has something for every type of traveler. Don't forget to explore the local culture and cuisine while you're there for a truly enriching experience!

Alona Beach is the most famous beach on Panglao Island, Bohol, known for its stunning white sand and vibrant atmosphere. It attracts both locals and tourists for its lively beachfront lined with restaurants, bars, and shops. The beach is perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports such as snorkeling and diving. It serves as a hub for island-hopping tours to nearby attractions like Balicasag Island.
