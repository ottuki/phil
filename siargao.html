About Siargao

Siargao Island in the Mindanao region has attracted surfers from across the globe thanks to the constant waves along its coast. While it's earned the nickname of the "Surfing Capital of the Philippines," the island has more to offer outside of riding the waves. For a more relaxed time, it's highly recommended to go island-hopping to visit the best Siargao beaches. The first stop in Siargao island hopping tours is Naked Island, which features a bare stretch of powdery white sand beach perfect for sunbathing. Another part of the famous trio of islands here is Guyam Island.

Guyam Island is a small, picturesque island located off the coast of General Luna in Siargao, Philippines. Known for its stunning natural beauty, white sandy beaches, and crystal-clear waters, Guyam Island is a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking a tranquil getaway. Guyam Island is a hidden gem in Siargao, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty, tranquility, and adventure. With its pristine beaches, stunning views, and relaxed atmosphere, Guyam Island is an ideal destination for travelers looking to unwind and enjoy the idyllic charm of the Philippines. Whether you're swimming, sunbathing, or simply enjoying the breathtaking surroundings, Guyam Island promises a memorable experience.
