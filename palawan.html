About Palawan

Palawan is the westernmost island in the Luzon region and is blessed with some of the most breathtaking beach hideaways in the Philippines. When planning a vacation here, there's more than a handful of Palawan beaches you can visit that are scattered across top municipalities like El Nido, Coron, San Vicente, Puerto Princesa, and Balabac. You can book a 2-week Palawan vacation package to explore most of these destinations. If you have less time, you can book a 1-week Puerto Princesa to El Nido tour package instead. The best beaches in El Nido include El Nido tourist spots like Nacpan Beach and Hidden Beach. Meanwhile, the must-visit Coron tourist spots for beach lovers are Malcapuya Beach and Banol Beach

El Nido, located in the northern part of Palawan, Philippines, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, crystal-clear waters, and dramatic limestone cliffs. Often referred to as one of the most beautiful destinations in the world, El Nido is a paradise for beach lovers, adventure seekers, and nature enthusiasts. El Nido, Palawan, is a tropical paradise that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, adventure, and cultural experiences. With its stunning landscapes, vibrant marine life, and a wide range of activities, El Nido is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking to experience the breathtaking wonders of the Philippines. Whether you're lounging on the beach, exploring hidden lagoons, or diving into the vibrant underwater world, El Nido promises unforgettable memories and experiences.
